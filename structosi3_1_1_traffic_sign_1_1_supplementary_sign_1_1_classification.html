enum osi3::TrafficSign::SupplementarySign::Classification::Type

Definition of supplementary sign types. See TrafficSign::Type for further information.

Enumerator
TYPE_UNKNOWN 

Type of supplementary sign is unknown (must not be used in ground truth).

TYPE_OTHER 

Other (unspecified but known) type of supplementary sign.

TYPE_NO_SIGN 

There is no supplementary sign (must not be used in ground truth).

TYPE_VALID_FOR_DISTANCE 

Traffic sign is valid for a certain distance (given by TrafficSignValue::value in e.g. meter - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).

TYPE_VALID_IN_DISTANCE 

Distance to the start of validity of the traffic sign (given by TrafficSignValue::value in e.g. meter - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).

TYPE_TIME_RANGE 

Time range for validity of traffic sign (starting from first TrafficSignValue::value and ending with second TrafficSignValue::value, both defining e.g. time of day in minutes since midnight - first TrafficSignValue::value_unit and second TrafficSignValue::value_unit). Day of the week starting from third TrafficSignValue::value and ending with fourth TrafficSignValue::value with third TrafficSignValue::value_unit and fourth TrafficSignValue::value_unit).

TYPE_WEIGHT 

Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign depending on the vehicle's weight (more than TrafficSignValue::value e.g. in kilogram - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).

TYPE_RAIN 

Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to wet / rainy conditions.

TYPE_FOG 

Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to foggy conditions.

TYPE_SNOW 

Restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to snowy / icy conditions.

TYPE_SNOW_RAIN 

Combined restriction of the validity of the traffic sign to rainy or snowy conditions denoted by a single supplementary sign (instead of two signs of TYPE_RAIN and TYPE_SNOW respectively).

TYPE_LEFT_ARROW 

Direction, left-pointing.

TYPE_RIGHT_ARROW 

Direction, right-pointing.

TYPE_LEFT_BEND_ARROW 

Direction of potential danger, left-pointing.

TYPE_RIGHT_BEND_ARROW 

Direction of potential danger, right-pointing.

TYPE_TRUCK 

Valid for heavy trucks.

TYPE_TRACTORS_MAY_BE_PASSED 

Passing, only tractors allowed.

TYPE_HAZARDOUS 

Hazardous.

TYPE_TRAILER 

Valid for vehicle with trailers.

TYPE_NIGHT 

Valid at night.

TYPE_ZONE 

Zone.

TYPE_STOP_4_WAY 

Stop 4 way.

TYPE_MOTORCYCLE 

Valid for motorcycles.

TYPE_MOTORCYCLE_ALLOWED 

Only motorcycles allowed.

TYPE_CAR 

Valid for cars.

TYPE_STOP_IN 

Ends in e.g. .. meter (given by TrafficSignValue::value in e.g. meter - TrafficSignValue::value_unit).
